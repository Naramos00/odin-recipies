Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is the perfect summer time dish. shrimp is cured in lemon and fresh onion, tomato, avocado, salt, pepper, jalapeno and cilantro are added to the mix. A perfect recipe for sea food lovers.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • 3 limes and 2 lemons
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced red onion
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado
  • Jalapeno
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Soak cleaned shrimp in 1/2 cup lime and 1/2 cup lemon juice for 30 to 60 minitues or until shrimp is opaque.
  2. Add in the tomatoes, onion, avocado, salt, pepper, jalapeno and cilantro to the shrimp and enjoy with tortilla chips or tostadas.
