Breakfast Burrito

Description

A sausage and egg burrito is a classic breakfast meal. loaded with sausage,egg and cheese all wrapped in a flour tortilla. Perfect for those in a rush.

Ingredients

  • Scrambled eggs
  • sausage
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage with onion, garlic, paprika, salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the scrambled eggs to your preference.
  3. Now to assemble the burrito. lay a flour tortilla down and add the scrambled eggs and the sausage. add your cheese and close up the burrito.
  4. Place burrito on a pan and cook until brown spots appear. finally serve with any sauce you like.
