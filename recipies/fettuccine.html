Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is an Italian dish containing a creamy sauce with garlic and fresh parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz. Fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 eg yolk
  • 2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. In a skillet melt butter and chopped garlic. Cook for 5 minutes while making sure not to burn the garlic.
  3. In a bowl add 1/4 cup heavy cream and egg yolk. Beat together and set aside.
  4. Pour the remaining cream into the skillet and increase the heat to medium-high. when the cream begins to boil mix rapidly with a whisk. Add the egg yolk and cream mixture to the skillet slowly and whisk until well blended to prevent curdling. Stir in one cup of parmesan cheese until combined.
  5. Add remaining parmesan cheese and parsley. Mix until smooth. remove from heat and serve over cooked pasta.
